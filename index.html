Team History

1995

Mega Livadi FC was founded by a group of local football enthusiasts.

2002

The club achieved promotion to the national second division for the first time.

2010

Mega Livadi FC won their first major trophy, the National Cup.

2018

The club moved to their new stadium, the Livadi Arena, with a capacity of 15,000.

2022

Mega Livadi FC qualified for European competition for the first time in club history.
